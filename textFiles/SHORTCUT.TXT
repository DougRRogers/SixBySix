To...

 Deal a new card...........................Ctrl+D
                             (or click on the deck)

 Move a card to the home pile
    ...double click on the card.
  
 Move a sequence of cards to the home pile
    ...double click on the topmost card in the
       sequence you want to move; the whole 
       sequence (up to the card you clicked)
       will go to the home pile (only if it is
       valid, of course!).
                                      
 Quit or Exit (same thing).......Ctrl+Q or Ctrl+X

 Start a new game..........................Ctrl+N
